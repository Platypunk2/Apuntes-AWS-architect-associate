Una NACL (Network Access Control List o Lista de Control de Acceso a la Red) es una capa de seguridad opcional en la nube (especialmente en Amazon Web Services (AWS)) que actúa como un firewall sin estado para controlar el tráfico entrante y saliente a nivel de subred. Regula el acceso basándose en protocolos, puertos y direcciones IP.


> [!NOTE] Explicación en Ingles
> [NACLs](https://translate.google.com/website?sl=en&tl=es&hl=es&client=srp&u=https://docs.aws.amazon.com/vpc/latest/userguide/vpc-network-acls.html) are used to controll access to network resources. They sit on subnets and evaluate traffic based on set rules, then determine whether or not that traffic should be allowed to continue. This applies traffic-based access control to your network, and is a powerful way to help secure your environment.
